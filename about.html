About The Flag

The reason Juneteenth is special to us because, slavery lasted for 400 years in the United State which is alot of years which now is more than 1,000 years ago. The Black Flag came from Hermosa Beach, California. The colors on the Flag stands for diffrent meanings/Reasonings.
